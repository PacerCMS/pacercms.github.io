Labor Day marks the ceremonial end of summer, and that means that most student publications are kicking back into high gear. We extend a special welcome to new staff members and wish you a successful year! There are a few other minor tweaks here and there throughout the codebase to improve performance and reliability.

This Release (Subversion R139)

  • Various bug fixes and minor enhancements from the Developer's List
