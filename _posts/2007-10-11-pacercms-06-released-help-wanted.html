We are happy to announce the latest stable release of PacerCMS, version 0.6. We thank all of you who provided feedback on our two pre-releases. As the project takes a step closer to 1.0 status, the time has come to renew our call for help from anyone able to contribute back to the code base. Our discussion list stands at more than 20 members, and every day we hear from users about their experiences. Whether your expertise is in design, PHP, javascript, database management, project promotion, language localization or all of the above, we can find a way to get you involved. For more details, simply join the developer's list and introduce yourself.
