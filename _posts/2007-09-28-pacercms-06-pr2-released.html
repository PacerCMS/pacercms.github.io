The latest preview release is now available for download. This release addresses minor bugs and increase compatibility with certain hosting platforms. This release is the first for our upcoming 0.6 release, which will feature a share article public module, the ability to use themed sections, adding and deleting sections in the Site Administrator, as well as various bug fixes and enhancements from our PacerCMS Developers list.
